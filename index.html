The Challenge

Navigating Healthcare Delivery Complexities

Healthcare providers frequently require quick guidance on standard practices outlined in national treatment guidelines. However, accessing these guidelines in real time is challenging due to the immense volume of documentation, strict time constraints, and the lack of accessible reference tools.

This challenge leads to inconsistencies in service delivery and uncertainty among frontline health workers. Furthermore, patients qualifying for regimen adjustments (like ARV) are often missed due to delayed communication between teams.

  • Fragmented Clinical Processes
  • Inaccessible Documentation
  • Delayed Patient Interventions

ClinAlert solves this.

By transforming fragmented workflows into a proactive, beautifully integrated system of care.

How ClinAlert Works

A streamlined workflow designed specifically for managing patients eligible for regimen switches, enhancing cross-team coordination.

1

Identify & Report

A healthcare worker identifies a patient eligible for a regimen switch. They use ClinAlert to instantly submit a structured incident report, capturing vital clinical details and the date of the next facility visit.

2

Automated Routing

Once submitted, the system automatically processes the data and distributes instant, targeted notifications to the required stakeholders, including program managers and pharmacists.

3

Prepare & Act

The clinical team receives the alert ahead of time. They prepare the necessary protocols and medications, ensuring a smooth, standard-compliant regimen switch during the patient's next visit.
